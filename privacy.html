Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy describes how SkillHour by Viswam Edutech collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our learning platform, including the web app, Windows desktop app, and mobile apps.

Information we collect

We may collect account details you provide (such as name and email), optional profile information, usage data to improve the product, and content you submit through the platform as part of your learning experience. School administrators may provision access for students and teachers through institutional accounts.

How we use information

We use this information to operate and improve SkillHour, personalize learning features, communicate with you about your account, provide offline downloads and streaming where enabled, and comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Sharing and storage

We do not sell your personal information. Data is processed to deliver the service to you and your school. Some information may be stored on your device (for example, offline OTT downloads) and on our servers when you sign in or sync activity.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@viswamedutech.com.
